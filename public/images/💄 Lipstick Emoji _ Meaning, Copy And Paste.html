Lipstick Emoji Meaning

Lipstick shown with the lid removed. Displays in a shade of red on most platforms, except WhatsApp and Twitter which have a more a pinkish color.

Lipstick was approved as part of Unicode 6.0 in 2010 and added to Emoji 1.0 in 2015.
